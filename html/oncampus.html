Bethune Residence
Price per room: $4500.00
Availability: Rooms Available
Location:

The Bethune Residence is the home for approximately 260 undergraduate students, the majority of them in first year. Our residence offers both tradistional style (floors 7-14) and suite style (floors 2-6) accomodation. In the traditional floors the rooms are both single and double occupancy with shared kitchenettes, bathrooms, and common rooms. The suite-style floors contain both 4 and 12-person suites, each with single and double bedrooms, a kitchen, bathroom, and one common room. Floor plans and a virtual tour are available.
