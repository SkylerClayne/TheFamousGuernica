How to Apply for On-Campus Housing

Residence at our Keele and Glendon campuses are guaranteed for first-year students who meet the housing application deadline on June 1. We will, however, continue to accept applications after the deadline. For more information, pricing, and available options please visit our On-Campus Housing page.

Submit your application for residence online through MyFile.

Step 1: Accept our offer of admission.
Step 2: Manage/complete your enrolment/course selection
Step 3: Pay your fees.
Step 4: Apply for residence.
Step 5: Explore your funding options and apply for financial assistance.
Step 6: Get the support you need.
Step 7: Get ready for first year!
